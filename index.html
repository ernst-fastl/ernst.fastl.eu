About Me

Love of learning
I love learning new things, whether on seminars/conferences, online, in books or through experimentation at work. I also like to share my knowledge, translate it for a particular corporate context and teach others by creating in house trainings and workshops or pairing with colleagues. Continuous personal improvement is essential for me.
Getting things done
I work hard to finish what I start. No matter the project or initiative I “get it out the door” in timely fashion. I don’t get distracted when I work and take satisfaction in completing tasks. Endurance, patience and commitment enable me to master challenges in the job as well as in my private life.
Humor and playfulness
I like to laugh and tease. Bringing smiles to other people is important to me. I put my colleagues at ease with my unyielding optimism and my humorous nature in high pressure situations. Creativity and innovation need a safe environment and cognitive ease. I create those circumstances for my teams so that they can thrive and flourish.
Social intelligence, communication and leadership skills
I am aware of the motives and feelings of other people. I know what to do to fit in to different social situations, and I know what to do to put others at ease. I can deescalate mounting conflicts and rekindle dull discussions. I encourage teams to get things done while preserving harmony within the group and making everyone feel included. I keep meetings factual and focused. I work in a prioritized and structured fashion and support others in doing so as well.
Professional excellence and software craftsmanship
I have high standards for professionalism in software craftsmanship and leadership. I my free time I facilitate coding dojos and code retreats. I am an active member of the Softwerkskammer and the Scrum User Group Austria.
Honesty, respect and fairness
Team spirit and healthy relationships are the basis for high performing teams and organizational units. For that trust is necessary. Trust is built on honesty, respect and openness among colleagues. I want to work in an organization where these values are taken seriously.
